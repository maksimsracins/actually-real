How it's different

Four independent signals, not one score

A trained AI-detection model, compression-artifact analysis (ELA), camera metadata, and Content Credentials (C2PA) — combined into one verdict.

A heatmap, not a black box

See exactly which regions of the photo triggered the result, with a plain-English explanation card for every signal.

Honest about uncertainty

Detection accuracy drops on screenshots, heavy compression, and edited photos — the app tells you when that's likely happening instead of hiding it.

What it checks

  • AI-generated images from tools like Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion
  • Missing or inconsistent camera metadata
  • Unusual compression artifacts common in synthesized or edited regions
  • Content credentials (C2PA), when present, that record an image's origin
